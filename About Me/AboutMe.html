Chat Application

The idea for my Chat Application project spawned quite randomly. While browsing a website , I was greeted with a chat display and a message stating, "Is there anything I can help you with?" It is a little early for me to endevor into an AI chatbot, however, the idea of a chatbox seemed reasonable. My sights were set. As my first project, this took a bit longer than expected, but I beleive this is due to the process of installing and organizing the correct software (Visual Studio Code, Visual Studio, MinGW, mySQL, etc.) I began this project in October of 2020 and finished* that same year in December.
